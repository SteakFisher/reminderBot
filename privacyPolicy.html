Our Privacy Policy


Believe it or not, I have no idea what to put inside a privacy policy, so I'll just tell you exactly what I use the user data I store for:

  • Access Tokens: Necessary for the bot to edit your Google Calendar.
  • Refresh Token: Necessary for the bot to refresh your Access Token.
  • Discord User Id: Necessary for the bot to correlate you to the data we have stored

For now that's it, I doubt I'll need to collect anymore personal data unless the bot blows up, but hey by then I may have a lawyer, and then I can wear the fancy suit and things.
